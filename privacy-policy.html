Privacy Policy:

BLOKSNKR respects your privacy. This application is designed to offer a personal sneaker collection experience, and we are committed to protecting your data.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information from users. You are not required to sign in, register, or provide any personal data to use the app.

Internet Usage

The app only uses an internet connection to retrieve sneaker data (such as names, images, and release info) from an external sneaker database. This is used solely to display information to you.

Local Storage

All the data related to your sneaker collection, favorites, and preferences is stored locally on your device. We do not upload, share, or sync this data to any server or third party.

No Social Features

This app has no social features, no messaging, no sharing, and does not connect with social media platforms.

Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, ads, trackers, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

Your Control

Since all your data stays on your device, you have full control. If you uninstall the app, all your saved data will be deleted.
